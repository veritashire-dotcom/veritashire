Why Offshore Hires Fail

The VA wasn't
the problem.
The process was.

70% of offshore VA hires fail within the first 90 days. Not because of talent — because no one built a structure around the hire. No onboarding, no communication rhythm, no oversight in the critical first weeks. The result: months of lost time and the belief that offshore hiring simply doesn't work.

It does. It just needs a system.

01

No role clarity before day one

Most VAs are hired based on a vague verbal brief. Without a written role definition, both sides are guessing within two weeks.

02

No communication structure

Without a daily standup rhythm, remote hires drift. Tasks slip. The founder re-explains everything. Disengagement follows.

03

Nobody watching weeks 1–3

Problems that would be caught in two days in an office take two months to surface remotely — by which point the relationship is already broken.

04

Wrong expectations on ramp time

Founders expect full independence in two weeks. The hires that work take six to eight. The gap is never discussed upfront.

The 90-Day Incubation

What actually happens
during the 90 days.

01
Weeks 1–2
Onboard

Your VA is set up on your tools, briefed on your workflow, and attending a structured daily standup. Every meeting is recorded and summarised by Fathom AI. We watch the first transcripts daily.

02
Weeks 3–6
Stabilise

Your VA begins taking ownership of recurring tasks. We monitor Slack and Fathom summaries for early friction signals. You meet us every fortnight for a 15-minute check-in. Any problem caught now costs nothing.

03
Weeks 7–10
Optimise

Scope expands. Task rhythm is set. Standup frequency reduces from daily to three times per week as independence increases. Performance review conducted. Transition brief prepared.

04
Weeks 11–13
Transition

Final performance report submitted. Buyout terms presented. Your VA's employment transfers to you directly. Payroll, Slack workspace, and all Fathom transcripts are handed over. We exit.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

If your question isn't here, ask it on the call. We answer everything.

What kinds of roles work with this model?

Any role involving recurring, defined tasks that can be managed remotely — executive assistance, operations support, inbox and calendar management, research, content coordination, customer service, data entry, and more. We'll tell you on the qualification call whether your specific role is a fit.

What if the VA doesn't work out?

One replacement is included within the 90-day incubation at no additional charge. Our system is designed to catch problems in weeks 1–3, not month 3 — which is why we monitor every meeting. If a replacement is needed, we act within 5 business days.

How much time do I need to put in during the 90 days?

You run one daily standup with your VA — 20 minutes, structured around four questions. Plus a 15-minute fortnightly call with us. That's the full commitment. We handle all the oversight behind the scenes.

Why do you charge a $2,000 buyout fee?

A standard recruitment agency charges $1,800–$2,400 upfront with zero post-hire support. You're paying $2,000 after 90 days of active oversight, daily monitoring, and a proven, stable hire. The value comparison isn't close.

What does upfront payment mean exactly?

Month 1 is invoiced in full when you sign. This confirms commitment and lets us begin your candidate search immediately. Months 2 and 3 are invoiced 5 days before each period starts. The buyout fee is invoiced at day 85. No surprises at any stage.

How does the timezone work for US/UK businesses?

Indian Standard Time (IST) is UTC+5:30 — a 9.5-hour difference from US Eastern, 4.5 hours from UK. Your VA typically works a schedule with 2–4 hours of morning overlap for US clients, and an afternoon overlap for UK. We align schedules before the hire starts — no guesswork.

What is the founder rate and how long does it last?

The founder rate of $999/month is available for our first 7 clients — in exchange for honest feedback and a brief testimonial at the end. The model and the level of oversight are identical to our standard offering. Once the 7 spots are filled, the rate moves to $1,400/month.
